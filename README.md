# AB-InBev-Internal-Environment-Analysis
# *Executive Summary*
This project analyzes AB InBev’s internal environment, focusing on HRM, diversity management, financial and non-financial performance, and overall strategic readiness. Using interviews, financial ratio analysis, and a full internal report, the project evaluates how AB InBev’s strengths and weaknesses enable it to respond to external opportunities and threats, supported by a structured SWOT and confrontation matrix.

# *Business Problems*
AB InBev faces high debt levels, potential HRM pressures, and diversity limitations, all of which affect financial flexibility, employee management, investment decisions, and long-term competitiveness in the global beverage market.

# *Key Questions*
•⁠  ⁠How does AB InBev’s debt affect liquidity, leverage, and competitiveness?  
•⁠  ⁠How do financial constraints impact workforce policies and HR practices?  
•⁠  ⁠What strategies can strengthen financial flexibility and HR effectiveness?  
•⁠  ⁠How do internal strengths and weaknesses influence AB InBev’s ability to respond to external market pressures?

# *Methodology*
•⁠  ⁠*Qualitative research:* 5 semi-structured interviews (Appendix 3).  
•⁠  ⁠*Operationalisation & coding:* Open + axial coding to derive patterns (Appendix 4).  
•⁠  ⁠*Financial analysis:* Profitability, liquidity, efficiency, leverage ratios compared with Heineken.  
•⁠  ⁠*Non-financial analysis:* Diversity, innovation, sustainability initiatives.  
•⁠  ⁠*SWOT & confrontation matrix:* Integrating all HR, financial, and environmental findings.

# *Skills Demonstrated*
•⁠  ⁠HRM and organizational culture analysis  
•⁠  ⁠Financial ratio calculation & benchmarking  
•⁠  ⁠Qualitative interview coding (open & axial)  
•⁠  ⁠SWOT & confrontation matrix development  
•⁠  ⁠Research methodology creation (sampling, operationalisation, ethics)  
•⁠  ⁠Report writing and evidence-based strategic advising  

# *Final Note*
This academic project (OP1 – Internal Environment Analysis) combines research, interviews, and financial evaluation to deliver strategic insights into AB InBev’s internal capabilities and improvement opportunities.
